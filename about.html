Delivery of Halal Food

In May 2011 Urinov Azizbek (the guy with the pizza) founded Delivery of Halal Food and began his journey of building the local leader in online food ordering. His founding team, completed by members of Team South Korea, shared decades of knowledge of working with internet companies. Likewise important, they shared a vision for the future of online food ordering. Together with a hand-picked, passionate team they managed to truly catapult the business to where it is today. Delivery Of Halal Food and its headquarters are located in Busan, boasting 100 employees in HQ alone. Diversity is a key pillar for our success. Employees with over 10 nationalities across 2 continents work for us, enabling us to exchange best practice from markets from each corner of the regions.
